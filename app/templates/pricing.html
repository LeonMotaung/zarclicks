Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

Can I change plans at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new rate will be applied immediately. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a contract or long-term commitment?
No, all our plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel your subscription at any time without any cancellation fees.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and for Enterprise plans, we also accept bank transfers.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits or startups?
Yes, we offer special discounted pricing for registered non-profit organizations and early-stage startups. Please contact our sales team for more information.
What happens if I exceed my plan's limits?
If you exceed your plan's campaign or influencer contact limits, we'll notify you and give you the option to upgrade your plan. For occasional overages, we may allow a grace period, but consistent overage will require a plan upgrade.
Do you offer custom plans for agencies?
Yes, we have special agency plans that allow you to manage multiple client accounts from a single dashboard. These plans include white-label options, client reporting, and additional team seats. Contact our sales team for agency pricing.
