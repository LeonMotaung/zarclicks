Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about API

How do I get started with 1nFloU?
Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for an account, complete your profile, and you can immediately begin exploring our influencer database or creating your first campaign. Our onboarding process includes a guided tour and access to helpful resources.
What types of businesses use your platform?
We serve businesses of all sizes across various industries including e-commerce, fashion, beauty, technology, food & beverage, travel, and healthcare. Our platform is flexible enough to meet the needs of startups, mid-sized companies, and large enterprises.
How do you ensure influencer authenticity?
We use advanced algorithms to analyze engagement patterns, audience demographics, and content quality. Our verification process includes checking for fake followers, engagement pods, and other inauthentic activity. We also provide performance history for each influencer.
Can I integrate with other marketing tools?
Yes, 1nFloU offers integrations with popular marketing tools including Google Analytics, Shopify, HubSpot, and social media management platforms. Our API also allows for custom integrations with your existing tech stack.
What kind of support do you offer?
We provide comprehensive support including email and chat support for all plans. Professional and Enterprise plans include priority support and dedicated account management. We also offer training resources, webinars, and strategy consultations to help you maximize your results.
